Pong Game

The Pong Game is a simple arcade game where two player controls two different paddles two hit a ball that moves through the screen. The players compete each other by preventing the ball to pass through their respective paddles. A player scores when the ball passes through the other player's paddle. The ball resets in the centre everytime a player scores.

Player 1 can control his paddle with the W and S keys on the keyboard. Player 2 can control his paddle with the up and down arrows in the keyboard.
